We would love for you to join the ESS and the English Department for the **Year End Celebration!**

This event will occur Tuesday **April 14th** in the **Student Union Ballroom B** starting at **4:30**.

This will include:

- An open mic reading session
- Free snacks and a cash bar
- A photo booth to capture memories with friends

The End of the Year Celebration is the perfect opportunity to socialize, destress, and commemorate all the hard work put in this term!

Don't miss out — we can't wait to see you there!
